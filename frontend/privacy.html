Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 11, 2026

Introduction

FinEdu is an educational financial platform providing tools, stock screeners, market news, and learning resources for investors. This policy explains how information is collected and handled when you use the platform.

Information Collected

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Seminar registrations
  • Usage analytics
  • Session data

How Data Is Used

  • Improve platform functionality
  • Send seminar reminders
  • Provide educational resources
  • Improve financial tools and screeners
  • Platform analytics

Data Protection

User data is stored securely with reasonable technical and operational safeguards. FinEdu does not sell user data to third parties.

Third-Party Services

FinEdu may integrate third-party services such as email services, analytics tools, and market data APIs to deliver platform functionality.

User Rights

Users may request data deletion and request access to account-related information by contacting the FinEdu team.
